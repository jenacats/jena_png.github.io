About Me

Hi! My name is Jena. I am a self-taught freelance illustrator based in Virginia. My art style varies between anime and semi-realistic with a focus on anime and video games I enjoy. I hope to continue to make fan art to share my love for these forms of media. Eventually in the future, I plan to expand my brand to include original works. Thank you so much for visiting my website!
