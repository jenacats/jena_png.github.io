Hi! My name is Jena. I am a self-taught freelance illustrator based in Virginia. While I am not drawing, I am a software engineer. My art style varies between anime and semi-realistic with a focus on anime and video games I enjoy. When I am not illustrating, I create hand painted press-on nails. The press-on nails I create are kawaii, practical, and affordable. Right now I do not have an online shop, but it is currently in progress. Thank you so much for visiting my website!
